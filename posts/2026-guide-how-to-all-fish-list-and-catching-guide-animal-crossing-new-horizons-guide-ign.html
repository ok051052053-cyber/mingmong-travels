Catching Fish by Location

Fish in Animal Crossing: New Horizons can be found in various locations like rivers, ponds, and the sea. Here’s a quick breakdown:

  • Ponds: Look for rarer fish such as koi in ponds which are typically more scarce.
  • The Sea: Many unique saltwater fish can be found here, making it a prime location for avid anglers.
