About MingMong Travels

MingMong Travels publishes clear, practical Korea travel resources for travelers in their 20s and 30s across the US and Europe. Our focus is simple: reduce planning stress and help you travel Korea with confidence.

What we do

We create beginner friendly guides that cover real travel decisions: where to start, what to pack, how to move around, and how to avoid common first trip mistakes. Content is written in plain English with checklists, quick explanations, and step by step suggestions.

What we cover

  • Korea Travel Experiences such as day trips, seasonal activities, and unique local experiences.
  • Korea Lifestyle and Essentials including SIM and eSIM, transport basics, budgets, and packing.

Editorial standards

We aim for accuracy and usability. Each guide is structured around practical questions travelers actually search for. When details can change, we recommend verifying official sources before booking or traveling.

Note: MingMong Travels provides informational content only and does not offer medical, legal, or insurance advice. Always confirm requirements and policies with official providers and local authorities.

Work with us

We collaborate with brands and local partners when the fit is genuinely helpful for travelers. We do not publish misleading claims. Sponsored content, if any, will be clearly disclosed.
